Q-An igneous rock is a rock that crystallizes from what?
A-melt
Q-Sedimentary rock can be turned into which of the three types of rock?
A-metamorphic rock
Q-When the three types of rock are re-melted what is formed?
A-a new magma
Q-What are the three major types of rock?
A-igneous, sedimentary, and metamorphic
Q-What changes the mineral content of a rock?
A-heat and pressure
