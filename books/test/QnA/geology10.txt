Q-Rock units become thicker and shorten when placed under this type of compression.
A-horizontal
Q-Where do thrust faults form?
A-In the shallow crust
Q-When rock folds deep in the Earth it can fold one of two ways, when it buckles upwards it creates what?
A-antiforms
Q-When rock folds deep in the Earth it can fold one of two ways, when it buckles downwards it creates what?
A-synforms
Q-If the tops of the rock units within the folds remain pointing upwards, they are called what?
A-anticlines and synclines
