Q-What principle relates to the formation of faults and the age of the sequences through which they cut?
A-cross-cutting relationships
Q-When rock formations are found on top of a fault that have not been cut, then they must be older or younger than the fault?
A-younger
Q-Finding what helps to determine if a fault is a normal fault or a thrust fault?
A-key bed
Q-Rocks on top of a fault that are cut are always older or younger than the fault itself?
A-older than the fault
